Dovizioso: the attendance king

The Italian, along with teammate Jorge lorenzo, has been competing on the World stage for 16 straight years - the third highest on the grid behind Valention Rossi (Movistar Yamaha MotoGP) and Dani Pedrosa (Repsol Honda Team). It's a huge testment to the 32-year-old, who in the last two years has been - on paper - Marc Marquesz' (Repsol Honda Team) most consistent challenger.
